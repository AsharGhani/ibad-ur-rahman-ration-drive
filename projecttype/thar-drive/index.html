Ration Procurement Plan
Tuesday, April 7, 2020
Sending assistance took longer, but Alhamdolillah ration procurement is this week as planned in sha Allah Some will be given month long ration, others for 15 days. Criteria: - Those that can find work, and assuming covid situation improves, then they can be back on earning track (2 weeks candidates). - Those that are not in a situation to find work, irrespective of the covid situation, will be served 1 month ration. That will help them for ramadhan too in sha Allah. Our ration is also seeing some changes as we took into consideration advices shared here ... whatever we could manage given the on-ground situation Will share that update too once ration is secured in sha Allah
